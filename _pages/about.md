I'm a Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) graduate student from BRAC University, Dhaka, Bangladesh. 

Currently, I'm serving as a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at BRAC University. I teach CSE220: Data Structures, CSE221: Algorithms, and CSE330: Numerical Methods.

Previously, I worked as a Lecturer in the Department of Information Technology & Management at Daffodil International University. During my undergraduate years, I also worked as a Student Tutor at BRAC University. 

I'm also pursuing an MSc in Computer Science and Engineering from BRAC University. My research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Image Processing.
